William Shakespeare

Born in Stratford-upon-Avon, April 26, 1564
Attended grammar school
Married at the age of 18

"A tiger's heart wrapped in a player's (woman's) body"

Published his first two long poems
Had his own theatre
Received a Coat of Arms (家徽)

Periods of Works

Experimental Period (1591-1593)
Titus Andronicus; Love's Labour's Lost; The Taming of the Shrew; The Comedy of Errors

Second Period (1594-1601)
Romeo and Juliet; The Merchant of Venice; A Midsummer Night's Dream; Much Ado about Nothing; The Merry Wives of Windsor; Julius Caesar

Third Period (1602-1610)
Hamlet; Macbeth; Othello; King Lear; Twelfth Night; All's Well That Ends Well; Antony and Cleopatra

Final Period (1611)
Cymbeline; Henry VIII; The Tempest
